The Problem Nobody Talks About

Even if you fixed the pipeline, you'd still hit the wall: your ideal client doesn't think they need you. The rejection isn't on price. It's on premise.

CEOs. Founders. Managing directors. People who built something from nothing. The business is their identity. When someone reaches out and says "I'm a business coach, and I'd like to help you grow your business," the reaction isn't curiosity. It's resistance.

"I've been running this company for fifteen years. Why would I need someone to tell me how to do it?"

This is rejection on premise, not on price or timing. Cold outreach that leads with coaching gets filtered before the message is finished.

Most agencies don't even know this wall exists. QCP was built around it.

They weren't pitched. They were featured. The rapport is already there. You're not selling. You're consulting. The COD call feels like a second meeting, not a first.

That's what a warm seat feels like. You sit down and the conversation is already halfway there. No cold start. No pitch. Just a prospect who already knows your name, already shared their challenges, and already chose to be in the room.

Every COD Call Is Pre-Qualified

Every COD Call Is Pre-Qualified. Here's Exactly How.

This is what separates QCP from every other agency you've spoken to. And it's the single biggest reason our partners' COD calls convert at significantly higher rates than typical outbound meetings.

Before any business owner reaches your diary, our team personally speaks with them. We don't book calendar slots. We run every conversation through the Team, Time, and Money qualification framework:

Team.

Does this business have the team size and structure that makes coaching relevant? We confirm they're not a solopreneur with no employees and no capacity to implement what you teach. Only businesses with real teams and real operational challenges make it through.

Time.

Is this person genuinely available and willing to engage? We confirm real intent, not polite interest. Not "maybe next quarter."

Money.

Does the business have the estimated turnover and budget to invest in coaching? We verify this directly, so you never sit across from someone who can't afford to say yes.

Then we brief them about you. Before the COD call, every prospect knows your background, the results you've achieved with other business owners, and exactly what the conversation will cover. They arrive informed, interested, and ready for a real business discussion.

Every booked COD call is meaningful, warm, and pre-qualified. Not just a name on your calendar.
